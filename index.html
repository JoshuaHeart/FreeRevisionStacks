Sample derivation from Topic A.1:

The displacement \( s \) can be found by integrating the velocity function \( v(t) = u + at \): $$s = \int_{0}^{t} (u + at) \, dt = \left[ ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2 \right]_{0}^{t} = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2$$
